Mango Salsa

A Recipes for a Mango Based Salsa

This mango salsa recipe is easy to make with ripe mangos, bell pepper, and lime juice. Jalapeños add some heat. It's so refreshing and tasty on fish tacos or with chips for dipping.

How to Make Mango Salsa

Mango Salsa Ingredents

  • Mango
  • Bell Pepper
  • Green onion
  • Jalapeños
  • Cilantro
  • Citrus juice

Instructions

  1. Peel and cut mango into chunks
  2. Chop Onions, and peppers
  3. Mix ingredents together in a bowl
  4. Serve and enjoy
