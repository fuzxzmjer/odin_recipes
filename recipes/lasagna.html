Lasagna

This is a recipie for Lasagna

Lasagna is a delicious pasta dish made of layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce

Ingredents

  • 1/2 lbs of ground beef
  • 1/2 lbs of ground pork
  • 1/2 cup of minced onion
  • 1 con of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped parsley
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of oregano
  • 1/8 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 package of Lasagna noodles
  • 1 Lb of small-curd cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup of parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 lb mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the ground pork and beef
  2. Add the ingredents for the sauce and flavor
    • Chopped onion
    • cans of tomatoes
    • half of the parsley
    • garlic
    • basil
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
    • oregano
    • sugar
  3. Cook the lasgna noodles, until al dente
  4. Mix the Cheese layer by combining the cheeses, pepper, salt, and parsley
  5. Assemble the Lasagna in layers as follows
    • Sauce
    • Pasta
